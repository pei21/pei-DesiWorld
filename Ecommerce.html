Product Review Flow Redesign

eCommerce Project

  • UX/UI Designer

Duration

  • Mar 2025 - April 2025

Tools

  • Figma | Jira | Lattice | Bazaarvoice

What is the problems?

Users often ignored the product review form due to poor Layout issues, a lack of clear structure, and the absence of a submission confirmation. Additionally, the review section plays a crucial role for customers when they are deciding whether to purchase a product. Therefore, it must be user-friendly, make it easy to check credible feedback, and provide a simple way to rate the item. A well-designed review experience can increase trust and influence potential buyers who are considering making a purchase.

The Goal

This project focused on improving the entire review section of an e-commerce platform. The goal was to make the review process more engaging, increase the conversion rate, and facilitate users in sharing their thoughts through a simple UI design, while ensuring it seamlessly integrated with the existing platform and maintained brand consistency.

Key Focus Area

  • Analyzing competitor review systems
  • Improving the existing review format
  • Redesigning the layout for better user navigation and interaction
  • Brainstorming up-to-date and modern review formats
  • Adding a sidebar summary for quick review insights
  • Implementing a size-fit review feature to help users understand how the product fits real customers

Competitor Analysis

I analyzed several competitor platforms with high review volumes to identify common strengths and weaknesses. Key factors included review volume (the number of reviews and engagement levels), user-friendly structure (how easily users could find key information such as overall rating and helpful reviews), and review presentation (how the reviews were displayed—star ratings, helpfulness, pros/cons). I discovered that many competitors had well-structured review sections and included visual guides that helped users quickly interpret the data, such as “80% of 1000 users recommend this product".

Survey Insights & User Behavior Analysis

I utilized surveys and other platforms to navigate user behavior and gain insights into pain points and expectations. Key findings included that users were overwhelmed by outdated long-form reviews and preferred concise summaries of the reviews. There was uncertainty about the meaning of the "overall rating," and users generally preferred a simple, interactive visual design where they could explore reviews in a more interactive way

Solution 1: Improved Review Format

The current format was enhanced by simplifying the review summary and breaking down key information into easily digestible segments (e.g., “80% of 1000 users recommend this product”). This allowed users to quickly assess product reputation without being overwhelmed by lengthy content.
