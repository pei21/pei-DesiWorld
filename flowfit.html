FlowFit - Mobile Made Simple

Project Type

  • Role
  • Product Designer

Duration

  • Jan 2025 - Mar 2025

Tools

  • Figma|Figjam
  • Canva
  • Adobe Suites

FlowFit is a digital wellness platform created to help individuals who may not have the budget or access to traditional gym memberships, yet still want to build and maintain a consistent, healthy lifestyle. The goal is to support people in nurturing their inner strength and improving their physical and mental well-being through online fitness classes, guided programs, and motivational tools.

Problems

Many people face challenges maintaining regular fitness habits due to expensive gym memberships, a lack of user-friendly online workout platforms, and struggles with consistency, motivation, and knowing where to start. FlowFit addresses these issues through an easy-to-use mobile app that offers weekly guided workouts, a supportive community, and clear workout paths. The project began with a gym branding identity and website to build trust and later expanded into a mobile app to provide more flexible and accessible fitness support.

Why an App?

  • 1. Mobile access to follow workouts on the go
  • 2. Daily and weekly workout planning
  • 3. Habit-building tools
  • 4. Encouragement through feeling and mental wellness.

Research

I conducted a survey to better understand user behavior and preferences. 47% of users want to improve their overall health‚Äînot just lose weight or gain muscle. 41% of respondents can only dedicate less than 30 minutes a day to fitness, and 52% are primarily motivated by improved energy and mood. These insights highlight the need for a fitness experience that is quick, energizing, and focused on total well-being.

In addition, 53% of users expressed a desire to train anywhere, showcasing the demand for flexibility. 35% struggle to find time, and 24% don‚Äôt know where to start, indicating the need for guided support. While 41% enjoy group challenges, others prefer to train alone. These findings validate the importance of building a flexible, motivational, and community-aware mobile app that adapts to diverse user needs and lifestyles.

Persona

Catherine is a 29-year-old freelance graphic designer who works remotely and often struggles to maintain a consistent fitness routine. She values mental wellness and wants to build a health habit that fits into her flexible but sometimes chaotic daily schedule.

Information Architechture

Using Catherine‚Äôs persona as a guide, I moved on to clarify the structure of information needed for the app. By understanding her behavior and key motivations, I created a clear information architecture that prioritizes task flow, such as joining a class, setting weekly goals, and tracking progress. This clarity ensures the app supports ease of use, builds consistency, and aligns with user priorities.

Journey Map

When it comes to user flow, for students, they start by navigating to the "Events" page, where they can browse through available events using filters like date, type, and location. Once they find an event they‚Äôre interested in, they can view the details and register by clicking the "Register" button. After registering, they receive a confirmation and a reminder closer to the event date.
