I'm a designer currently based in Toronto

I am a designer with a background in art and several years of experience working in team environments. Throughout my academic journey, I’ve learned how to break down projects from concept to final product, and I’ve developed the ability to clearly communicate my design process. For this reason, I focus on turning ideas into human-centered products.
