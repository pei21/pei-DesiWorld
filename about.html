About Me

I'm a versatile designer currently based in Toronto

With a background in Multimedia Design and Development, I focus on human-centred design to improve digital life. I have experience in video editing, branding, and UX design — not only making life easier for users but also collaborating with businesses to achieve shared goals.

After years of working on solving customer problems, I transitioned my career to User Experience (UX) Design. Since then, I’ve worked on improving websites, mobile apps, and digital products.

As a nature lover from a beautiful island, I’ve grown with resilience and a strong growth mindset. This unique origin has made me open to different cultures and adaptable to diverse environments. My journey through Australia, Asia, and Ireland eventually led me to Canada, where I now contribute my skills.
