I'm a designer currently based in Toronto

I am a UX designer with a background in visual design and hands-on experience in customer-centered environments. In my recent project with Roots, I demonstrated strong collaboration and communication skills while working with cross-functional teams to design and deliver user-centered solutions. My academic journey has equipped me with the ability to define problems, conduct user research, and translate insights into intuitive interfaces. I take pride in clearly articulating my design process and presenting outcomes that balance both user needs and business objectives.

Over the years of working in various industries, I transitioned my career to User Experience (UX) Design in 2020. Since then, I’ve designed websites, mobile apps, and even physical products. My strong attention to detail ensures a consistent layout design and has helped enhance usability, reducing task completion time by 15% across mobile and web platforms through user testing. These improvements have ultimately led to increased client satisfaction. I’m passionate about turning ideas into human-centered, accessible, and impactful products.

I’m from Taiwan, a tiny but beautiful island that has shaped me to be resilient and positive in the world I live in. Thanks to this unique origin, I’m open to different cultures and able to adapt to diverse societies—something I’ve experienced while living in Australia, Asia, Ireland, and eventually landing in Canada, where I now contribute my skills. Coffee is my favorite drink—it helps me stay focused during the week and gives me a moment to relax in a cozy local café on the weekend.
